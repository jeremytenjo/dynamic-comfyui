ComfyUI Commands

Use these from a terminal in this container.

1. First-time setup or full update
   Command: bash /install.sh
   What it does: Installs/updates ComfyUI, installs required custom nodes and models, enables ComfyUI-Manager modern UI, and starts ComfyUI.
   When to use: First run, after image/template updates, or when you need to refresh installed dependencies.

2. Restart ComfyUI service only
   Command: bash /restart-comfyui.sh
   What it does: Stops and starts ComfyUI, then waits for health check success.
   When to use: After changing runtime settings or if ComfyUI becomes unresponsive.
   Note: Does NOT reinstall models or custom nodes.
